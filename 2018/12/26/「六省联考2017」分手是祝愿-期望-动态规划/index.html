dp[i]={i(i<=k)1+indp[i−1]+n−indp[i+1](k<i≤n) dp[i]= \begin{cases} i &(i <= k)\\ 1 + \frac{i}{n}dp[i-1]+\frac{n-i}{n}dp[i+1]&(k<i\le n) \end{cases} dp[i]=​⎩​⎨​⎧​​​i​1+​n​​i​​dp[i−1]+​n​​n−i​​dp[i+1]​​​(i<=k)​(k<i≤n)​​
